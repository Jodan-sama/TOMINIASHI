§02 — PROCESS
1 / CAST
Glass is hand-blown over graphite moulds in the atelier's south furnace. Each shell is imperfect on purpose.
2 / FILL
A proprietary orange plasma — silicone-suspended ferrofluid with a phosphor dopant — is vacuum-pumped into the core.
3 / TUNE
Capacitive sensors and piezo contacts are calibrated to the resonant signature of the specific glass vessel.
4 / BREATHE
The instrument runs on its own for 72 hours in the dark before shipping. It begins to breathe.
